Last updated: March 2026

About this Site / Data Controller (Impressum)

This site is operated by Dimea Arcade, operated by Dimitri Dähler. Contact: contact@dimea.audio. This section serves as the Impressum for Swiss legal purposes.

What Data We Collect

We do not directly collect personal data beyond what is provided voluntarily (for example, a newsletter signup). Data is collected by the third-party processors listed below when you make a purchase or sign up for the waitlist. We do not operate our own database of customer personal data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party processors. Each collects data under their own privacy policy:

  • LemonSqueezy (Merchant of Record) — processes plugin purchases and collects your name, email address, and payment details. LemonSqueezy handles VAT collection and payment compliance on our behalf. LemonSqueezy Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — processes hardware deposit payments and collects your name, email address, and payment details. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Mailchimp — manages newsletter and waitlist signups. Collects your email address only. Mailchimp Privacy Policy
  • Coinbase Commerce — processes cryptocurrency payments (BTC, ETH, USDC, SOL) and collects wallet address and transaction data. Coinbase Commerce Privacy Policy

Analytics (Plausible — Pending)

We plan to use Plausible Analytics, a cookie-free analytics tool that collects no personal data and requires no consent banner. No analytics tracking is in place at launch. This section will be updated when Plausible is activated.

Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies on this site. Third-party processors (LemonSqueezy, Stripe, Mailchimp, Coinbase Commerce) may set their own cookies when you interact with their services. Please refer to each processor's privacy policy for details.

Your Rights (GDPR / Swiss nDSG)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data held by our processors. To exercise these rights, email contact@dimea.audio with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

These rights apply under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nDSG / FADP, in force since September 2023).

Legal Basis for Processing

Processing is based on:

  • (a) Contract performance — when you purchase a plugin or place a hardware deposit
  • (b) Legitimate interest / consent — for newsletter communication, which you explicitly opt into
  • (c) Legal obligation — where required by Swiss or EU law

Policy Updates

We will notify Mailchimp subscribers by email when this policy is significantly updated. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
