   Email [contact@dimea.audio] with:
   - Your order number or proof of purchase
   - The product (Arcade Chord Control or DIMEOLA)
   - Brief reason (optional for plugin, not required)

   We will confirm receipt within 2 business days.

7. **Processing Time** — h2 section:
   Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and returned via your original payment method. Cryptocurrency refunds (Coinbase Commerce) may take additional time depending on network conditions and will be returned in the same currency if possible, or as USDC.
