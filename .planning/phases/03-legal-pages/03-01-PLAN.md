# Phase 03 Plan 01: Legal Pages

## Objective

Create the three legal pages required by LemonSqueezy and Stripe before payments go live: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Refund Policy.

## Requirements

### 03-01a: Privacy Policy
- GDPR-compliant privacy policy at `/privacy.html`
- Covers data collection, cookies, third-party services

### 03-01b: Terms of Service
- Terms of Service at `/terms.html`
- Covers license, usage rights, liability

### 03-01c: Refund Policy
- Refund Policy at `/refund.html`
- No-questions 14-day refund for plugin
- Hardware deposit non-refundable with clear notice
