# Phase 06 Plan 01: Warm Analog Audio Engine

## Objective

Transform the joystick XY pad from a digital-sounding raw oscillator engine into a warm, lush, analog-feeling synth pad — inspired by the Elka Synthex and Oberheim OB-8.

## Requirements

### 06-01a: Waveforms
- Sine + triangle only — sawtooth and square removed from random pool
- Root voice alternation: sine/triangle (was sine/square)

### 06-01b: Filter
- Sweep range reduced: ~800Hz (centre) to ~2500Hz (edge)
- Filter never fully opens — stays warm throughout

### 06-01c: Envelope
- Attack: ~30ms soft ramp
- Release: 1s fade on chord lift

### 06-01d: Reverb + Delay
- Plate reverb ~25–35% wet, algorithmic (Web Audio nodes)
- Old reverb tail fades ~200ms on chord change
- Delay: ~200ms, dark filtered feedback (~1200Hz), 3 decaying repeats, always on
